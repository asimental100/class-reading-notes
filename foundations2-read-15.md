In 2014, 70% of the employees at the top tech companies in Silicon Valley, such as Google, Facebook, and Twitter, were male.

Marketing towards men, that sometimes involved using anti-female gags/rhetoric, helped to create this divide. Unless something changes then "advertising will continue to drive a subtle message to girls and women that it’s not a place where they belong.”
